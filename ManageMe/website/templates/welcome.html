ManageMe

How to Use ManageMe

  1. Create an Account or Login to existing account.
  2. Add your tasks for the week.
  3. Choose start and endtimes for your tasks.
  4. View your calendar to see what to do and when.

Tips and Tricks to Stay on Task

  1. Set clear goals and break them down into smaller steps.
  2. Prioritize important tasks and tackle them first.
  3. Use a task management tool like this site.
  4. Create a schedule and stick to it as much as possible.
  5. Minimize distractions by turning off notifications.
  6. Take short breaks every hour or so to avoid burnout.
  7. Practice mindfulness to stay focused on the present.
  8. Work in short focused bursts.
  9. Stay hydrated and well-rested to maintain focus.
  10. Reward yourself for completing tasks.
